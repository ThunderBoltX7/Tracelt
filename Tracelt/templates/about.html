What is Tracelt?

Tracelt is a modern lost & found marketplace where people can report lost items, found items, and even buy/sell goods. Our platform connects communities to help recover lost belongings and facilitate safe transactions.

Why This Exists

Losing personal items is stressful and frustrating. Tracelt aims to make the process of recovering lost items easier and more efficient. Additionally, we provide a marketplace for buying and selling goods within your community.
